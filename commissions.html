Felting/Doll Commissions

Commissions for felted art dolls and collectibles for your original characters/animals, favorite characters, or a personally designed critter by me. Pieces range in size from just a few inches to nearly a foot in each dimension.

Pricing

6" or less: $65 for quadraped characters/creatures $75 for bipedal characters 8" or greater: $90 for quadraped characters/creatures $100 for bipedal characters Additional shipping cost not included (pieces are lightweight but may need a descent sized box) (tips appreciated!)
